Chicken and rice

Desciption

This is an easy one-pot recipe saving you from the hastle of washing a ton of dishes! This meal is comforting and family friendly. Great served with green beans, broccoli, or a salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 6 (4 oz) bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 1 ½ tablespoon ground black pepper
  • ⅓ cup finely chopped yellow onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 ½ cups uncooked long-grain rice
  • 3 cups chciken stock, divided, or more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped flat-leaf parsely
  • 1 medium lemon, cut into wedges
